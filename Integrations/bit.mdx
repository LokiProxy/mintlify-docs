---
title: "BitBrowser"
---

Before integrating proxies into BitBrowser, please ensure your proxies are properly configured and you have obtained the necessary connection parameters.

1. Open BitBrowser and click **"Create Profile"**.
2. In the **"Proxy Settings"** section, enter your proxy details:
   - If using API-extracted IPs: Enter the **Host** and **Port** copied from LokiProxy.
   - If using username/password authentication: Also fill in the **Username** and **Password** fields.
3. Click **"Test Proxy"**. Once the test succeeds, confirm and save to start using the proxy.
