---
title: "Hubstudio"
---

Before integrating proxies into Hubstudio, please ensure your proxies are properly configured and you have obtained the necessary connection parameters.

1. Open Hubstudio and click **"New Environment"**.

2. Navigate to **"Basic Settings"**, select your **"Proxy Type"**, and enter your proxy details:

   - If using API-extracted IPs: Enter the **Host** and **Port** copied from LokiProxy.

   - If using username/password authentication: Also fill in the **Username** and **Password** fields.

3. Click **"Check Proxy"**. Once the connection is verified, click **"Finish"** to start using the proxy.
