Before using our service, please register an account. This account will automatically serve as your first username/password combination for accessing our proxy services.

First, visit the LokiProxy website and click the "Sign up" button in the top-right corner of the homepage.

On the registration page, enter your email address and a 6-12 character password containing both letters and numbers. Then click the "Create account" button to complete registration.

We will send a confirmation email to your address. Click the link in the email to activate your account.

After successful activation, you will be redirected to the LokiProxy dashboard to begin your journey.
