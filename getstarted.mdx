Before using our service, please register an account. This account will automatically serve as your first username/password combination for accessing our proxy services.

1. First, visit the LokiProxy website and click the "Sign up" button in the top-right corner of the homepage.
2. On the registration page, enter your email address and a 6-12 character password containing both letters and numbers. Then click the "Create account" button to complete registration.
3. We will send a confirmation email to your address. Click the link in the email to activate your account.
4. After successful activation, you will be redirected to the LokiProxy dashboard to begin your journey.
