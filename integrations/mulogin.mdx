---
title: "MuLogin  "
---

Before integrating proxies into MuLogin, please ensure you have properly configured your proxies and obtained the necessary connection parameters.  

1. Open MuLogin and click **"Add Browser"**.  

2. Enter a profile name, go to the **"Basic Configuration"** section, select **"Proxy Settings"**, and enter your proxy details:  

   - If using API-extracted IPs: Enter the **Host** and **Port** copied from LokiProxy.  

   - If using username/password authentication: Also fill in the **Username** and **Password** fields.  

3. Click **"Check Network"**. Once the connection is verified, click **"Save"** to start using the proxy.
