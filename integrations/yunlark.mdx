---
title: "Yunlark  "
---

Before integrating a proxy into Yunlark, please ensure you have properly configured your proxy and obtained the necessary connection parameters.

1. Open Yunlark, go to the **"Store Management"** section, and click **"Create New"**.

2. Select the **"Account Platform"** and **"Proxy Type"**.

3. Enter your proxy details:

   - If using API-extracted IPs: Enter the **Host** and **Port** copied from LokiProxy.

   - If using username/password authentication: Also fill in the **Username** and **Password** fields.

4. Click **"Check Proxy"**. Once the connection is verified, click **"Confirm"** to start using the proxy.
